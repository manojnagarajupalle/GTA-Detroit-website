About Our Chapter

Our Mission & Vision

The Global Telangana Detroit Chapter's mission is to serve as a vibrant hub for the Telangana diaspora in Metro Detroit, preserving and promoting our cultural identity, empowering our community through various initiatives, and facilitating strong connections among members.

We envision a thriving community where Telangana traditions are celebrated, new generations are engaged, and collective efforts lead to significant local impact. We strive to be a beacon of cultural pride and community support in Michigan.

History of the Detroit Chapter

The Global Telangana Detroit Chapter was established in [Year of Establishment] by a group of passionate individuals who recognized the growing need for a dedicated platform for the Telangana community in Metro Detroit. Starting with a few enthusiastic members, the chapter has grown steadily, organizing numerous successful events and becoming a cornerstone for cultural activities and community support.

Over the years, we have celebrated every major Telangana festival, hosted educational workshops, organized charity drives, and provided a strong network for new immigrants. Our journey is a testament to the strong spirit and collaborative efforts of the Telangana diaspora in Detroit.
