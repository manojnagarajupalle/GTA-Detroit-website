Who We Are

The Global Telangana Detroit Chapter is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Telangana within the Metro Detroit area. We are a proud extension of Global Telangana, uniting Telanganites and fostering strong community bonds.

Our mission is to serve as a central hub, organizing cultural events, educational programs, and community service activities. We celebrate our traditions, empower our members, and build lasting friendships, ensuring the spirit of Telangana thrives here in Detroit.
